Explore and Drink Wine

We analzyed over 100,000 wines to build machine learning (ML) algorithms to predict wine quality. First, we used Tableau to do learn and understand the date. You can explore these visualizations below.

You can also play with the machine learning models. Choose features of a wine to be given a predicted quality as well as 3 of the best wines that fit your choices. Or, play wine critic and see how our ML model will rate a wine based on a description you write.

Find your perfect wine!

Use dropdowns to select year, varietal, region, and enter a maximum price. You'll get a prediction for how good this wine should be, and you'll also be presented with the three best wines that match your choices from a database of 100,000 wines.

Be a wine critic!

Create your own wine description below, and get a predicted wine score ranging from 80-100, based on a machine learning algorithm.
